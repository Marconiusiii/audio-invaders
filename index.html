How to Play

  1. Wear Headphones.
  2. Listen for beeps moving Left to Right.
  3. Click FIRE when the sound is in the CENTER.
  4. Faster beeps = Aliens closer to bottom!
  5. Keyboard Support: Fire the cannon with Space Bar or Enter Key. Press "e" to hear current Energy, "s" to hear current score, and "r" to hear current round when using a screen reader.
  6. After Round 5, hitting 3 aliens in a row will give you a +15 energy boost. After round 5, max energy tops out at 175.
