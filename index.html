How to Play

  1. Wear Headphones.
  2. Listen for beeps moving Left to Right.
  3. Click FIRE when the sound is in the CENTER.
  4. Faster beeps = Aliens closer to bottom!
  5. Fire the cannon with Space Bar or Enter Key.
  6. After Round 5, hitting 3 aliens in a row will give you an energy boost.
