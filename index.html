How to Play

How to Play

  1. Wear Headphones.
  2. Listen for Alien beeps moving Left to Right.
  3. Click FIRE when the sound is in the CENTER.
  4. Faster beeps = Aliens closer to the bottom!
  5. Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • Fire the cannon with Space Bar or Enter Key
    • Press "e" to hear current Energy.
      • Press "s" to hear current score.
    • Press "r" to hear current round.
    • Press "p" to pause and unpause the game.
    • Press "v" to toggle between the verbosity options.
  6. After Round 5, hitting 3 aliens in a row will give you a +15 energy boost. After round 5, max energy tops out at 175.
  7. Runners start spawning at Round 10. They sound different from the Alien beeps, move fast, are worth 200 points and +20 Energy, but you lose 30 Energy if they hit the ground. Beware!
